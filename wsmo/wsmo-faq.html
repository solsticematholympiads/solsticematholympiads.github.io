WSMO FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
Below are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about WSMO.
When will WSMO be held?▼
The 2025 WSMO will start at 11AM EDT on Sunday, December 21, 2025.
What rounds does the WSMO consist of?▼
WSMO consists of four rounds:
  • Speed Round: 10 questions in 30 minutes
  • Accuracy Round: 10 questions in 60 minutes
  • Team Round: 15 questions in 60 minutes
  • Tiebreaker Round: 3 questions in up to 30 minutes (only for breaking ties)
The Speed, Accuracy, and Tiebreaker rounds are taken individually. The Team Round is taken collaboratively, and teams are responsible for managing their own communication during the contest.
What type of questions does the WSMO have?▼
WSMO questions are short answer format. All answers are guaranteed to be integers.
What is the difficulty of the WSMO?▼
WSMO difficulty ranges from early AMC 8 to late AIME. For a better idea of what to expect, check out our problems on the Past Contests page.
