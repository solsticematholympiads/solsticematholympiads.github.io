2023 WSMO

After a brief pause in 2022, the Winter Solstice Math Olympiads (WSMO) made a strong return on December 21, 2023.

Students from across the U.S. and beyond participated in the renewed edition, solving original problems and collaborating in team rounds. Generous sponsors, a dedicated team, and an enthusiastic group of competitors helped make the 2023 WSMO a memorable comeback.
