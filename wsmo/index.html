Winter Solstice Math Olympiad

The inaugural Winter Solstice Math Olympiads (WSMO) was held virtually on December 21, 2021 - the winter solstice. Initially launched by a small team of middle school students, WSMO 2021 marked the very beginning of our competition series, designed to inspire creative problem solving across all levels of experience.

Dozens of students from across the country participated in our first-ever contest. Prizes were awarded to top individuals and teams, generously supported by our sponsors.
