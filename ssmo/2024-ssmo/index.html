2024 SSMO

The 2024 Solstice Summer Math Olympiads (SSMO) was held on June 22, 2024 over ZOOM.

Participants received the Zoom link before the contest began. Problems were administered through PDFs—one for each section, and one per round for the relay section. All PDFs and their corresponding Google Form submission links were shared in the Zoom chat during the contest.
