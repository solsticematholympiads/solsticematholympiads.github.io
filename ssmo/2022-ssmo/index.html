2022 SSMO

The Solstice Summer Math Olympiads (SSMO) launched its very first contest on June 27 2022, kicking off a new tradition of collaborative, student-led math competitions.

Students from across the U.S. and internationally joined the debut edition, solving creative problems in both individual and team rounds. With the support of passionate organizers and early sponsors, SSMO 2022 set the stage for what would become a growing solstice tradition.
