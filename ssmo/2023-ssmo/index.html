2023 SSMO

The 2023 Solstice Summer Math Olympiads (SSMO) was held from June 24 to June 26, 2023, using gclocked, a timed Google Form platform.

Competitors could begin submitting at 12:00 AM EST on Saturday, June 24, with the submission window closing at 11:59 PM EST on Sunday, June 25. Results were finalized by 2:00 PM EST on Monday, June 26, followed by a mandatory tiebreaker round on Zoom at 4:00 PM EST. Final rankings were released before 7:00 PM EST, and prizes were sent out afterward.
