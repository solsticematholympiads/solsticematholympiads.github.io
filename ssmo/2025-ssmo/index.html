2025 SSMO

The 2025 Summer Solstice Math Olympiads (SSMO) was held on June 21, 2025 over ZOOM.

Participants recieved the ZOOM link before the contest began. Problems were adminstered through password-protected PDFs for each round. All PDFs and their corresponding Google Form submission links were shared in the ZOOM chat during the contest.
