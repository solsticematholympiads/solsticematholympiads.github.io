SSMO FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions
Below are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about WSMO.
When will SSMO be held?▼
The 2025 SSMO has already taken place. Check back later for information regarding the 2026 SSMO!
What rounds does the SSMO consist of?▼
SSMO consists of five rounds:
  • Speed Round: 10 questions in 30 minutes
  • Accuracy Round: 10 questions in 60 minutes
  • Team Round: 15 questions in 60 minutes
  • Relay Round: 3 problems in 10 minutes, completed as a team. The answer to each problem is needed to solve the next.
  • Tiebreaker Round: 3 questions in up to 30 minutes (used only to break ties)
The Speed, Accuracy, and Tiebreaker rounds are taken individually. The Team and Relay rounds are taken collaboratively, with teams managing their own communication.
What type of questions does the WSMO have?▼
SSMO questions are short answer format. All answers are guaranteed to be integers.
What is the difficulty of the WSMO?▼
SSMO difficulty ranges from early AMC 8 to late AIME. For a better idea of what to expect, check out our problems on the Past Contests page.
