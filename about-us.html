About Us

Mission Statement
At Solstice Math Olympiads, our mission is to inspire high school students through our biannual math competitions.

We challenge students on our team by creating thought-provoking questions and sharing them with other students.

We are committed to creating challenging, engaging, and creative problem-solving experiences for learners at all levels.
