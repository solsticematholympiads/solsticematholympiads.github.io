Contest Rules

Eligibility:
All students 18 years old or younger are eligible to participate in the contest.
Allowed Materials:
Students may only use writing utensils, blank scratch paper, rulers, protractors, compasses, and erasers. No calculators or electronic devices are allowed, other than those used expressly to access the contest platform(s) (e.g., Zoom, Google Forms, PDF of the contest, communication with teammates during team portions). No questions will require the use of calculators.
Integrity:
Any participants found cheating will be immediately disqualified from the current and any future competitions and will not be eligible for any prizes. The participant's team will also be disqualified from the current competition and will not be eligible for any prizes.
Appeals:
Appeals may be made to solsticematholympiads@gmail.com. Appeals will not be recognized after the contest period has ended.
