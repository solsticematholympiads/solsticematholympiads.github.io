Frequently Asked Questions
Below are answers to some of the most frequently asked questions about WSMO.
Are contests free?▼
Yes! All SMO contests — both WSMO and SSMO — are completely free for everyone.
Who can participate?▼
SMO contests are open to participants aged 18 and under. If you're older, you're still welcome to explore our problems on the Past Contests page.
Where are contests held?▼
All SMO contests are held virtually. Participants join a Zoom meeting to receive the problems and submit their answers through a provided Google Form.
